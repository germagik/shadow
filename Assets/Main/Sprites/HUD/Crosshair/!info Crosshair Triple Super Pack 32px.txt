Hello and welcome!

This super pack contains 110 unique crosshair graphics in 3 different easy-to-use formats!

Formats:
PNG on a solid black background, intended as a mask to determine alpha, aka Black = invisible, White = visible.
PNG on a transparent background, to be used as is. Crosshair itself solid with no transluscency at all.
PNG on a transparent background, to be used as is. Crosshair itself partially transluscent. <<< This is the one you're most likely going to use.

Image Size: 
32x32px

Use them in any 3D or 2D game either as a crosshair to show a character's aim or to use it as a replacement graphic for the mouse cursor.
